How It Works

Four simple steps to turn your surplus food into someone's nourishment.

1
List Your Surplus

Describe the food items, quantity, and pickup location from your event in under 2 minutes.

2
Nearby Match

Our system instantly connects you with verified NGOs and recipients in your area.

3
Coordinate Pickup

Arrange a convenient time for food collection. We handle the logistics seamlessly.

4
Make an Impact

Receive confirmation and a live report showing exactly how your donation helped.

Why ReLink
Built for real impact

Every feature is designed to make donating as frictionless and trustworthy as possible.

Real-time
Instant Matching

Our algorithm finds the closest verified recipient to your pickup point within seconds of listing.

Verified Network

Every NGO and donor is manually verified. You always know who you're working with.

Impact Dashboard

Track your donations, see meals shared, and get a full report on your contribution over time.

Zero Waste Mission

Every item shared reduces landfill waste and carbon emissions. We calculate your environmental savings with each donation and display it on your profile, giving you a tangible measure of your contribution to the planet.

Zero Platform Fee

ReLink is completely free. 100% of your donation value reaches those who need it most.
